## Quantum Simulation Idea

The Simulation entangles two players' circuits using the J Gate, applying strategies, then untangling the J Gate to produce the classical reward cases. 

The strategies are currently Long, Short, Q (representing a Quantum Strategies) and the py file showcases all scenarios and payoffs for Chicken and for Prisoner's Dilemma. 

## Objectives

1. Possibly implement various scenarios not restricted to just Long, Short, or Q to see the payoffs from a quantum circuit 
2. Apply more than 2 players in a circuit to see the various outcomes related to the benefits of Quantum Entanglement

Furthermore, try to test out various circuits along with various gates to make quantum circuits easier to understand.
